Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2025

SupplyPoint Vault respects your privacy. This app is designed from the ground up to prioritize your data control and security.

1. No Account Required

SupplyPoint Vault does not require you to create an account or sign in. No personal information is collected or stored externally.

2. Local-Only Storage

All your firearm, ammo, and range data is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload, transmit, or sync your information to any cloud or server.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

This app does not use analytics tools, trackers, or cookies of any kind. Your usage behavior is not monitored or shared.

4. Permissions

Some permissions (like camera or file access) are requested only for specific features like taking firearm photos or importing backups. These are optional and used locally only.

5. Backups and Exports

You may choose to export or back up your data manually. Files you generate are saved to your own device or storage location of your choosing.

6. Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy or the app, feel free to reach out: questions@supplypointvault.com
